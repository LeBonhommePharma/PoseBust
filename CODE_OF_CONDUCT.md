# Code of Conduct

PoseBust is a scientific tooling project. We keep the community technical,
respectful, and focused on correctness.

## Our pledge

Participation should be a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless
of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Argue about code, licenses, and science — not people.
- Use direct, honest language without dehumanizing anyone.
- Assume good faith; fix mistakes in public technical discussion when possible.
- Credit prior work and respect license boundaries (see `docs/licensing/`).

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or targeted insults.
- Deliberate derailment, trolling, or spam.
- Sharing private information without consent.
- Submitting code known to violate the project’s Apache-2.0 / clean-room policy.

## Reporting

Report issues via GitHub or to the maintainers listed in `CONTRIBUTING.md`.
Maintainers may warn, restrict, or ban contributors at their discretion.
